## Storage and Shelf Life

Proper storage of green Maeng Da kratom preserves potency, prevents contamination, and extends usability. Understanding optimal storage conditions helps maintain product quality over time.

### Best Storage Practices

**Airtight containers**: Exposure to air oxidizes alkaloids, reducing potency. Store kratom in:
- Vacuum-sealed bags
- Glass jars with tight lids
- Food-grade plastic containers
- Original packaging if properly sealed

**Cool, dark locations**: Heat and light degrade alkaloid content. Ideal storage areas include:
- Kitchen cabinets away from appliances
- Closet shelves
- Basement storage (if dry)
- Refrigerator or freezer for long-term storage

**Moisture prevention**: Humidity promotes mold growth and clumping. Keep kratom:
- Away from sinks and water sources
- In climate-controlled environments
- With silica gel packets for extra protection
- Out of bathrooms and laundry areas

### Shelf Life Expectations ‚è∞

**Powder form**: When properly stored, kratom powder maintains potency for:
- 1-3 months at room temperature (optimal quality)
- 6-12 months in cool, dark conditions
- 1-2 years when refrigerated or frozen

**Capsule form**: Pre-filled capsules have similar shelf life to powder but may experience gelatin degradation:
- 3-6 months at room temperature
- 1 year in cool storage
- Gelatin capsules may become brittle over time

**Signs of degradation**:
- Color change (darkening or fading)
- Unusual odors
- Clumping or moisture presence
- Reduced effects when consumed
- Visible mold or discoloration
